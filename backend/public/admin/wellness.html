Mind & Wellness

Train your mind like you train your body.

Mindfulness Practices

Start your day with 5 minutes of deep breathing or gratitude journaling to set a positive tone.

Mental Fitness

Read books, learn new skills, and limit negative self-talk. Replace “I can’t” with “I’m learning.”

Self-Care Tips

  • Take 15-minute nature walks
  • Unplug from social media regularly
  • Prioritize 7–8 hours of sleep
  • Talk to a friend or therapist when overwhelmed
