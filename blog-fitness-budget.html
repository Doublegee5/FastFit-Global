Fitness on a Budget

Fitness doesn’t need to be expensive. Here are smart ways to stay in shape without draining your wallet:

  • Bodyweight Workouts: No gym? No problem. Pushups, squats, and planks build real strength.
  • Cook at Home: Simple meals like oats, eggs, rice, beans, and vegetables save money and fuel gains.
  • Use Free Resources: YouTube, free apps, and local parks can support your full routine.
  • Track Progress: Stay motivated with a notebook or app to measure your improvements.

You can train smart, eat well, and stay consistent—without expensive memberships or supplements.
